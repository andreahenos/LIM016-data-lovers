Ghibli Corner is intented to provide information about the studio's films and Ghibli films and their details to fans or people interested in discovering more about their projects.

Foundation Studio Ghibli was founded in 1985 by directors Isao Takahata, Hayao Miyazaki, and Toshio Suzuki.

Movies There are 22 movies, but there is an additional one that is considered to be Miyazaki's first film.

Awards 6 of his films are the highest grossing in Japanese cinema and 5 have been nominated for an Oscar.
